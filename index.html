About Me

Hi, my name is Logan. I'm a mechanical engineer in product development and robotics out of Vanderbilt University. These days I've been at an integration partner in the space industry developing all the diverse mechanical systems needed to run experiments autonomously on the ISS. I love this kind of work because I get to build something new every day, and often it's something that I've never seen until the day before.

If you've found your way here, don't be afraid to reach out and say hello. I'm always looking for new projects!
